Bubble "Slide to Unlock" alternative

This is the demo for the "bubble" concept. The goal is to provide an alternative functionality to "Slide to Unlock" with the benefit of authentication.

How it works?

When you press and hold you'll notice that a bubble starts growing from the center of the screen, when you let go it shrinks. You'll also notice some rings sorrounding the bubble. Each ring turns the bubble a different color when the bubble reaches that ring. In order to unlock the phone, you must use this mechanism to provide the pattern set as the password.

How to do the pattern?

Expand the bubble to the first color in the pattern, then let go until the bubble shrinks to the next color, repeat until all colors in the pattern are selected
